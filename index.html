Hi there !

Curious, imaginative and passionate, I like to discover new things and in particular, the scientific world in general. Since I was very young, I have wanted to work in the space sector and it was with this in mind that I wanted to work as apprentice in this field during my training at UTC. It is during this 3-year experience that I worked in satellite pre-projects on innovative projects such as CHIME (ESA's Copernicus program for hyperspectral imaging), Daedalus (ESA's atmospheric analysis project), EnVision (ESA's project to study Venus) or on ESPRIT for the Gateway (space station around the Moon). I realized different tools (in Python with Jupyter notebooks or in MATLAB) in order to perform studies of these projects (using MBSE principle).

This experience gave me the opportunity to study and work on the Blockchain technology, applied to space applications in Singapore, within the S4TIN (Smart Small Satellite Systems Thales in NTU) service in 2019.

Beside that, art is a field that fascinates me. In this context, I made short films in high school, I do a lot of photography since 2018 and I am working on a video game project in order to discover and use the Unreal Engine 4, as well as Blender.

Convinced of the importance of space exploration for science and the understanding of our environment, I want to continue to bring my competences in this sector.
